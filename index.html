Biography

The Early Years

I was born in Tenessee and lived there until I was 4 years old. During that time my parents adopted my younger brother, James. We then moved to Myrtle Point, Oregon. It is a little logging community between Roseburg and Coos Bay. We lived there until my 10th birthday. I attended kindergarten, first and second grades at the local schools. Third grade I was homeschooled as we prepared to move to Africa.

My younger brother Brent was born while we were there.

Africa

The summer I turned 10, my family moved to Ndola, Zambia, in southern Africa. We spent 5 years there with a year and a half back in Oregon between years 3 and 4. I graduated high school from a boarding school in Kenya.

My younger sister Maria was born there.

Army Years

After high school I returned to the United States. I did a year of college and then joined the Army. I spent 5 years on Active Duty and spent time in California, Texas, Arizona, Georgia, South Carolina, and Bosnia. After Active Duty I joined the California National Guard. I was with the Guard for 3 year, and deployed to Iraq during that time.

Portland Til Now

After Iraq I moved back to Portland. I met the man who is now my husband a few months after arriving. We have a house on the east side which we share with two small dogs - Tux and Gumdrop.
